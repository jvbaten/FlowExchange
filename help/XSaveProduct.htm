Product stream

Connecting the product stream to the XFlowSaver unit operation is optional.

If the product stream is connected, it will be set equal to the feed stream. For this, at least the properties "Flow Rate" and "Composition" or the property "Compound Flow Rate" must be present in the property configuration.

The product stream will be flashed using the recommended auto-flash method. The properties required for this flash must also be present in the property configuration.

For the TP flash, "Pressure" and "Temperature" must be part of the property configuration. For the P-VF flash, "Pressure" and "Vapor Fraction" for the overall phase or "Phase Fraction" for each phase must be present. For the PH flash, "Pressure" must be present and the Write Enthalpy option must be checked in the XFlow file options.
